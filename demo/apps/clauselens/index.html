Legal disclaimer

ClauseLens is an AI-assisted research tool that helps a person locate contract language for a first read. It is not a substitute for qualified legal counsel and does not give legal advice.

AI extraction can be incomplete, imprecise, or wrong. A clause marked Not Found may still exist in a form the model did not recognize. Verify every extracted clause against the original document before acting on it.

Contract text is sent to the AI provider for analysis and is not stored by this tool. This demo uses a fictional contract. Do not paste a real, sensitive contract into a public tool.
